Transition property

Suppose we want to make an animation for few seconds. Then we will be adding 'transition-property: x' where x is the property (like width, height, etc) that you want to animate. Then, add 'transition-duration:y' and I don't think that I need to explain what this means. Below is the box, animated!

There are properties in transition too!

  1. transition-delay - This is used to delay an animation before starting.
  2. transition-timing-function - This one's important and is used to control the speed of animation in between of entry and exit. Suppose you want the animation to be fast in the starting and then gradually slow it down during the end, then this property is the key to that lock.
  3. transition - It's a shorthand property which goes like 'transition: tp du ttf td' where tp is 'transition-property', du is 'transition-duration', ttf is 'transition-timing-function' and td is 'transition-delay'.
